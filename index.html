The War
Comes Home.

Since February 2022, Russia has launched over 38,000 recorded strikes across Ukraine. What began as a military campaign — targeting airfields, bases, and supply lines — shifted into a systematic assault on civilian life. Over 3.6 million people remain internally displaced. By mapping where strikes concentrate and where displaced populations gather, we can identify protection priorities and evacuation corridors that save lives. Data: ACLED, IOM DTM Round 20, KSE Institute.
