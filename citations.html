Sources and Limitations

Strike Data — ACLED (Armed Conflict Location & Event Data Project). 38,564 Russian strike events downloaded from ACLED's Ukraine Conflict Monitor, covering February 24, 2022 to april 16. Events were filtered to Russian strikes and categorized by target type (energy, residential, healthcare, education) using keyword matching against event descriptions. Categorization is approximate since not all strike descriptions contained necessary information. Strikes in occupied areas may be underreported due to limited media access. acleddata.com

Displacement Data — IOM Displacement Tracking Matrix, Round 20 (April 2025). IDP and returnee estimates per oblast, downloaded from the UN Humanitarian Data Exchange. All figures are exactly as published by IOM. Donetsk, Luhansk, Kherson, and Zaporizhia oblasts have no IDP estimates because IOM surveys cannot reach occupied or active combat zones. data.humdata.org

Oblast Boundaries — Natural Earth Admin 1 (1:10m). Cartographic approximations; may not reflect exact administrative borders. naturalearthdata.com

Military Air Bases — Coordinates for 14 major Ukrainian airfields scraped from individual Wikipedia articles using Claude; positions may be offset by 0.5–2 km. Base selection prioritizes bases struck in the early days of the invasion.

Border Crossings — Border crossing locations were identified by hand from official border crossing lists and UNHCR-reported refugee routes. Geographic coordinates were not provided by these sources and were manually derived by locating each crossing point in OpenStreetMap and Google Maps and getting latitude/longitude coordinates.

Proposed Humanitarian Response Plan — Evacuation corridor calculation, aid zones, and medical center locations are proposed interventions, not real facilities or official plans. Corridors were derived from where we saw least strike density and IDP flow data. We marked aid zones in areas where high strike counts overlap with IOM data gaps.

Basemap — CARTO Dark Matter tiles. Map rendering by MapLibre GL JS (v4.7.1).
